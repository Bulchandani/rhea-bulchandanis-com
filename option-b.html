A Note from the Maker

Stories worth telling.

I make things — short films, written work, performances — that explore characters, ideas, and the small moments that don't always get told. The work tends to start with a feeling, then becomes a question, then becomes something you can watch or read.

A proper site is on the way, and it'll have all of it organised. While I build it, the YouTube channel is where you'll find what I've been working on. Some of it is finished, some is rough — both kinds get put up.

Most of what I make starts with a small thing
nobody else seems to notice.
